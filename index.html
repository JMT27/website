About Me

Hi, I’m James Tanuwijaya, a Computer Science undergraduate at BINUS University specializing in Cybersecurity. I have a strong interest in ethical hacking, penetration testing, digital forensics, and reverse engineering. Over the past year, I’ve gained hands-on experience in assessing the security of web and mobile applications.

My goal is to become a cybersecurity professional who not only identifies vulnerabilities but also strengthens systems to be more secure and resilient. I am seeking a Cybersecurity Internship where I can apply my knowledge, gain practical industry experience, and contribute meaningfully to protecting digital environments.
